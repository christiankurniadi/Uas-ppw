About Us

Founded in 2022, Cigs is the Ultimate Cigarettes dan Vapes E-commerce catalog and resource center. Our goal is to offer the largest dedicated selection of smoking devices, the fastest ship speeds, and best customer service in the industry.

Cigs subsidiaries provide employment for 2,000 people at the beginning of 2022. As a "big family", the welfare of employees is our priority, from safety standards to the provision of sports and health facilities.

Training in leadership, managerial, administrative, and technical skills is also held from time to time to both inside and outside company.
